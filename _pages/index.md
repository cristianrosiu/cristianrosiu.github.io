#  About Me

I’m a **Rendering Engineer** at **Ubisoft Bucharest**, where I specialize in bringing immersive visuals to life for the **Assassin’s Creed** brand. With a background in **GPU programming** and **real-time rendering**, I’m passionate about crafting optimized, visually compelling experiences that push the boundaries of what’s possible in gaming. My work spans the intricacies of rendering pipelines, shader optimization, and cross-platform development, with a strong focus on leveraging cutting-edge technology to enhance performance and fidelity.

Outside of my role, I’m passionate about deep-diving into the latest **research** in graphics and rendering, seeking out novel solutions and sharing insights with the community. I’m driven by the challenge of bridging cutting-edge research with practical, impactful applications in gaming. My goal is to foster understanding and inspire others who are as fascinated by the technical artistry behind game development as I am.
